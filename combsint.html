Combs and the Int construction

After seeing my MSc colleague Daphne Wang working on Kissinger and Uiljen's combs for the whole term, I realized at some point that these were lenses in a compact closed category. The proof goes as follows. 

 ∫ᵂ (A → W ⊗ B)×(C ⊗ W → D)
≅ ⟨ Duals ⟩
 ∫ᵂ (A → W ⊗ B)×(W → C* ⊗ D)
≅ ⟨ Yoneda ⟩
 A → (C* ⊗ D) ⊗ B
≅ ⟨ Duals, symmetry ⟩
 I → A* ⊗ B ⊗ C* ⊗ D

The same proof can be used to show that Int(C)≅Optic(C) when C is compact closed. Here Int is the Int construction.

In the general case, after a suggestion by Jules that came from a tweet by Piedeleu; Elena Di Lavore and me wrote down an explicit Optic(C)→Int(C) embedding.
